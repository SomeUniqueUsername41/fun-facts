Random fun facts

  • People born in the 1950's may be old, but they were born a little closer in time to the future year of 2126 AD than to the year 1768; 1768 and 1950 are 182 years apart while 1950 and 2126 are 176 years apart, 1768 and 1951 are 183 years apart while 1951 and 2126 are 175 years apart, and so on.
  • Comfortable rest for centaurs in transportation would rely on full-body support rather than traditional seating, using padded chest rests to stabilize the upper body while wide slings or harnesses support the horse-like lower half. The floor would include soft, slightly recessed standing bays to keep hooves steady and reduce strain, while gentle suspension helps absorb motion so all four legs can rest without constant balancing. Open rear space and adjustable rails would prevent restriction and allow natural alignment, letting centaurs travel in a supported standing posture that feels stable, relaxed, and low-impact over long distances.

  • The correct name for a wishbone is "furcula".
  • To hypothetical aliens living 100 million lightyears away, dinosaurs are still roaming Earth. This is because light from Earth takes 100 million years to reach a planet that's 100 million lightyears away from Earth.

  • After being in the dark, your pupils widen and your retina becomes more sensitive to capture more light, so when the room suddenly gets bright, too much light floods in at once; squinting is a reflex that quickly reduces the amount of light entering your eyes by narrowing the opening and helps your eyes adjust safely.
